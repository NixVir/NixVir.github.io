    Privacy Policy

    Effective Date: February 16, 2026

    NixVir LLC ("NixVir," "we," "us," or "our") is a data analytics and technology consultancy based in Colorado. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our website (nixvir.com) and our professional consulting services.

    1. Information We Collect

    Website Analytics. When you visit nixvir.com, we collect standard analytics data such as pages visited, referring URLs, browser type, and approximate geographic location. We do not collect personally identifiable information through our website. We do not use contact forms, email signup forms, or any mechanism that requests personal data from visitors.

    Client Engagement Data. In the course of consulting engagements, clients may provide NixVir with business data, operational records, financial information, or other datasets necessary to perform contracted services. This data is provided under the terms of individual service agreements.

    2. How We Use Information

    • Website analytics are used solely to understand site traffic patterns and improve site content.
    • Client data is used exclusively for the purposes defined in the applicable engagement agreement, such as analysis, reporting, and deliverable preparation.

    3. Data Sharing

    We do not sell, rent, or trade any information to third parties. Client data is never shared outside the engagement team without explicit written authorization from the client. Aggregated, non-identifying analytics data may be used internally to improve our services.

    4. Client Data Protection

    • Confidentiality. All client data is treated as confidential and handled in accordance with the terms of the applicable service agreement.
    • Access Controls. Client data access is limited to personnel directly involved in the engagement.
    • Retention. Client data is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill the engagement and any contractual obligations. Upon project completion, or promptly upon the client's request, data is returned or securely destroyed at the client's direction.
    • Security. We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

    5. Cookies and Tracking

    Our website uses standard analytics cookies to measure site traffic. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing tools. Our analytics implementation respects browser Do Not Track signals.

    6. Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage visitors to review the privacy policies of any external sites they visit.

    7. Children's Privacy

    Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

    8. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
